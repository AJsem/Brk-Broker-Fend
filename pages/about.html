Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got you covered. Check out our FAQ section for answers to common questions about AitTrade and how to get started.

Is my money safe?

AitTrade implements robust security measures to protect user funds and personal information. We utilize bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and cold storage for the majority of our cryptocurrency holdings.

How do I get started?

Getting started with AitTrade is simple. Sign up for an account, verify your identity, deposit funds, and you're ready to trade. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly for both beginners and experienced traders.

What kind of cryptocurrency does AitTrade support?

AitTrade supports a wide range of popular cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Ripple (XRP), and many others. We continuously evaluate and add new cryptocurrencies to our platform based on user demand and market trends.

How long do deposits and withdrawals take?

Deposit and withdrawal times can vary depending on the method used and the cryptocurrency being transacted. Generally, deposits are processed instantly, while withdrawals may take a few hours to a few days to complete.
